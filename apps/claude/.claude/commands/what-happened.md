---
description: Summarize recent activity from recording
---

Get context and provide a summary of what happened recently.

## MCP Resource

Fetch `context://all/recent` from the `pair-programmer` server.

## Response

Analyze the context and provide:
1. **Timeline** - What happened in chronological order
2. **Key actions** - Important things the user did
3. **Current state** - What's happening now
4. **Notable items** - Errors, decisions, or important details

Keep it concise and actionable.

---
description: Get audio transcriptions from recording
---

Fetch audio transcriptions (mic and system audio) from the recording via MCP resources.

## MCP Resources

Fetch both resources from the `pair-programmer` server:
- `context://mic/recent` - User's microphone transcriptions
- `context://system_audio/recent` - System audio transcriptions

## Response

Summarize what's been said:
- User's spoken words (mic)
- Audio from apps/videos (system audio)
- Key points and instructions mentioned
